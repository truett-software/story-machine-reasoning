Kim Kardashian and boyfriend Pete Davidson attend premiere of reality series

The couple were all-smiles as they held hands outside the Goya Studios in Los Angeles

1/12Los Angeles Premiere of Hulu’s ‘The Kardashians’ Show

By
Tina Campbell
08 April 2022

Kim Kardashian was joined by boyfriend Pete Davidson as she hit the red carpet for the premiere of her family’s new Hulu show, The Kardashians.

The reality star, 41, and her Saturday Night Live star beau, 28, were all-smiles as they held hands outside the Goya Studios in Los Angeles on Thursday.

Kardashian made sure that all eyes were on her in a form-fitting silver gown by Thierry Mugler featuring a daring thigh cut.

She accessorised with a statement choker.

The couple are thought to have been dating for around six months.

Davidson is said to have supported his girlfriend amid her ongoing divorce from rapper Kanye West.

The Kardashians premiere

The Kardashians will premiere exclusively on Disney Plus on April 14 in the UK.

It serves as the follow-up to the famous family’s long-running show Keeping Up With The Kardashians, which ran for 20 seasons.

Khloe Kardashian also attended the premiere with three-year-old daughter True in her arms.

Kris Jenner wore a pink dress and was seen with her boyfriend Corey Gamble.

Kourtney Kardashian also put in an appearance with Travis Barker following their surprise Las Vegas wedding last Sunday.

They were joined by her children Penelope and Mason.

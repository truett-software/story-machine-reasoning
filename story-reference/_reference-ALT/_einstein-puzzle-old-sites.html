Inferencing is process of deriving additional information from data. A reasoner is a software program that performs inferencing. A reasoner can check accuracy and consistency of an ontolology. Examples: verify that an electronic food label is consistent with a food labeling ontology; identify subsumption relationships (i.e. "is_a" relationship); check the content of a triple store.
